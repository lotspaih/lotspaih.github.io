Hello! I'm Mark Lotspaih.

I'm a long-time self-taught Technology Generalist and recent Project Manager. Currently, I work in the area of public education as a Systems Administrator and Technology Project Manager helping to support, plan, and integrate technology into classroom instruction and learning. I've also worked in Healthcare and I.T. Consulting for the areas of Information Security and Accounting. I advocate for and support "digital privacy, free speech, and innovation" online as a member of the Electronic Frontier Foundation.

I have a B.Sc. in Information Systems Technology and several technology and project management certifications: A+, Network+, Security+, Linux+, Project+ (CompTIA), Certified Wireless Technology Specialist (CWNP), and Certified Project Manager (IAPM). I've managed budgets, schedules, and scopes for multiple information technology projects such as systems and data migrations, network and wireless infrastructure upgrades, software updates and installations, and resolving day-to-day operational inefficiencies. I like scripting process/workflow automations, data conversions, and system configurations using Python and Powershell.

Presently, I am researching and studying Agile project management methodology, tools, and frameworks, including Scrum, DevOps, Lean, and Kanban.

Those who know me personally know that I'm just an "ordinary average guy"" who enjoys spending time with family and friends, reading too many books, hiking and Nordic walking around the Great Lakes region, and supporting my local communinity.
